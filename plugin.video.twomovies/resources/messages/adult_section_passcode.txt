Adult Passcode Controls allow you to lock Adult Rated videos. Enabling this function means that,
depending on your settings, you will be asked for a PIN before entering an Adult Rated Section.

Adult Sections can be disabled all together in the Addon Settings area. 

This DOES NOT mean that all ADULT videos will be blocked, as this addon
pulls videos and information from public websites where videos are submitted by 
random users and the information connected to a video may not be accurate,
Therefore it is impossible to be 100% sure all Adult content willbe filtered.
